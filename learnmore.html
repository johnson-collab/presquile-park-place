‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How fast do the scooters go?

Our scooters have a top speed of 20 km/h, which is perfect for park trails and safe for all riders.

How far can I go on a single charge?

Our scooters can travel approximately 15-20km on a full charge, more than enough to explore the entire park!

What if I have mechanical issues?

Call us immediately at (613) 403-2297 and we'll assist you. Don't attempt repairs yourself.

Can I take the scooter outside the park?

No, scooters must stay within Presqu'ile Provincial Park boundaries and designated trails only.

What happens if it rains?

Please return the scooter early if weather turns bad. We'll credit unused time for a future rental.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Contact us for special rates for groups of 4 or more scooters.
